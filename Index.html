About Me

Giesel Arnold, BSc, RYT-200, the founder of Giesel Yoga, received her yoga teacher certification from the Institute of Yoga in Houston Texas in 2014. Giesel has been practicing yoga since 2009 and specializes in Vinyasa Krama Yoga, which is a flowing sequence of asanas/postures linked by breath and intention.
